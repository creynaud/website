About me

Hi, my name is Claire. This is my personal website. I am a mobile and cloud software engineer with 8 years experience. Above all I like to build software that brings value to its users. I am also a big fan of Lean Start-up and Agile methodologies like Scrum. I have been working for startups (Trango Virtual Processors, acquired by VMware) as well as big companies (VMware, Yahoo). I am currently working for Epyx in Lausanne, Switzerland.

Personal projects

Right now, I am building an iOS localization service called BabelBytes.


On BabelBytes, iOS app developers can get their app translated by native speakers.


This project is keeping me up at night for several reasons:

  • 1. I have been tearing my hair out trying to get apps translated for too long.
  • 2. I am very interested in experimenting on the Minimal Viable Product concept, from the Lean start-up methodology
  • 3. I wanted to try out a cloud PAAS provider with a real project (BabelBytes is running on Heroku. It also uses Amazon S3 for file storage.)
  • 4. I needed to improve my web development skills, because in my opinion such skills are now mandatory for a mobile developer
