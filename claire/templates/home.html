About me

Hi, my name is Claire. This is my personal website. I am a mobile and cloud software engineer with 8 years experience. Above all I like to build software that brings value to its users. I am also a big fan of Lean Start-up and Agile methodologies like Scrum. I have been working for startups (Trango VP, acquired by VMware) as well as big companies (VMware, Yahoo). I am currently working for Epyx in Lausanne, Switzerland.


I think Software Engineering is an amazing craft, and I do care about my craft. I try to improve my skills all the time, by learning from others or on my own. I am also a loud speaker and I always speak my own mind based on my rational analysis.


Personal projects

Right now, I am working on an iOS localization service called BabelBytes.


On BabelBytes, iOS app developers can get their app translated by native speakers. Anyone can become a translator, provided that he speaks English fluently.


This project is keeping me up at night for several reasons:

  • 1. I am very interested in experimenting on the Minimal Viable Product concept, from the Lean start-up methodology
  • 2. I wanted to try out a cloud PAAS provider with a real project (BabelBytes is running on Heroku. It also uses Amazon S3 for file storage.)
  • 3. I needed to improve my web development skills, because in my opinion such skills are now mandatory for a mobile developer
